Places to Visit in South Korea

Seoul

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace: A historic palace and popular tourist destination.
  • N Seoul Tower: Offers panoramic views of the city from the top.
  • Bukchon Hanok Village: Traditional Korean houses and a glimpse into the past.
  • Myeongdong Shopping Street: A bustling area for shopping and street food.

Busan

  • Haeundae Beach: Famous beach known for its beautiful sands and clear waters.
  • Gamcheon Culture Village: A colorful, artistic neighborhood with unique architecture.
  • Jagalchi Fish Market: Korea's largest seafood market with a wide variety of fresh seafood.
  • Beomeosa Temple: A historic Buddhist temple set in the mountains.

Ulsan

  • Jangsaengpo Whale Museum: A museum dedicated to whales and maritime history.
  • Ulsan Grand Park: A large urban park perfect for outdoor activities and relaxation.
  • Daewangam Park: A scenic coastal park with beautiful rock formations and views.
